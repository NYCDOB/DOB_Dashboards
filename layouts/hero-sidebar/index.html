Permit Types by Community District (2016)
Summary of Permit Types Issued & Renewed (2016)
NB Permits

Brooklyn had the greatest number of NB permits issued and renewed in 2016 (2,604 total, 35% citywide). The Community District with the greatest number of NB permits issued and renewed in 2016 was CD503 in Staten Island (518 total, 7% citywide)

ALT1 Permits

Brooklyn had the greatest number of ALT1 permits issued and renewed in 2016 (2,975 total, 35% citywide). The Community District with the greatest number of ALT1 permits issued and renewed in 2016 was CD407 in Queens (404 total, 5% citywide)

ALT2 Permits

Manhattan had the greatest number of ALT2 permits issued and renewed in 2016 (61,187 total, 50% citywide). The Community District with the greatest number of ALT2 permits issued and renewed in 2016 was CD105 in Manhattan (14,884 total, 12% citywide)

ALT3 Permits

Manhattan had the greatest number of ALT3 permits issued and renewed in 2016 (10,669 total, 44% citywide). The Community District with the greatest number of ALT3 permits issued and renewed in 2016 was CD105 in Manhattan (1,778 total, 7% citywide)

DM Permits

Brooklyn had the greatest number of DM permits issued and renewed in 2016 (862 total, 38% citywide). The Community District with the greatest number of DM permits issued and renewed in 2016 was CD407 in Queens (135 total, 6% citywide)

Total Permits

Manhattan had the greatest number of total permits issued and renewed in 2016 (75,109 total, 45% citywide). The Community District with the greatest number of total permits issued and renewed in 2016 was CD105 in Manhattan (17,204 total, 10% citywide)

Job Type per Year (2011 - 2016)
On average, A2 job types account for 74% of permits issued and renewed per year. A2 job types for permits issued and renewed have been trending up over the past six years. In 2016 there was a 34% increase in A2 job types for permits issued and renewed compared to 2011. While DM job types account for the least amount of permits issued and renewed for each year of the analysis period, the numbers have been trending up (there was an 90% increase in 2016 compared to 2011).
Issued & Renewed per Year (2011 - 2016)
Permits issued and renewed have been trending up over the past six years. In 2016 there was a 32% increase in permits issued and renewed compared to 2011. While the number of permits issued has been greater than permits renewed for each year of the analysis period, there was a slight decrease in the proportion of issued permits in 2016 (2 percentage points less than in 2015 and in 2011).
Job Type per Borough (2011 - 2016)
The greatest proportion of job types within each Boroughs was A2 with Manhattan having the greatest number overall (54% of A2 job types citywide). The greatest number of NB job types issued and renewed occurred in Brooklyn (32% of NB job types citywide). DM job types represent the smallest proportion of job types within each Borough. Queens had the greatest number of DM job types issued and renewed (34% of DM job types citywide).
Issued & Renewed per Borough (2011 - 2016)
The Borough with the greatest number of permits issued and renewed was Manhattan accounting for 49% of permits issued and renewed citywide. Manhattan had the greatest number of permits issued (49% of permits issued citywide) as well as the greatest number of permits renewed (47% of permits renewed citywide). While Staten Island had the least number of total permits issued and renewed, the proportion of permits that were issued was the greatest out of all Boroughs (73%).
Job Type: Top 10 Community Districts (2011 - 2016)
CD105 in Manhattan had the greatest overall number of permits issued and renewed and the greatest number of A2 job types citywide. CD503 in Staten Island had he greatest number of NB job type permits citywide. CD407 had the greatest number of DM job type permits citywide.
Permits Time Series (2000 - 2016)
The greatest year-to-year increase in total initial and renewed permits occurred from 2003 to 2004. During that time New Building permits increased by 20% and Demolitions increased by 27%. This upward surge in permitting activity continued through 2005, but was followed by a six year period of declining New Building and Demolition permits which both dropped almost 70%. However, since 2013, New Building and Demolition permits have again steadily increased by 40%. The overwhelming majority of permits issued since 2000 are for ALT 2 permits which are for minor alterations in which the use or occupancy of a facility does not change.
Demolition Permits Density (2016)
Demolition Permits Cluster Example: Willets Point, Queens
Demolition permits were filed in July 2016 for 8 buildings in what is referred to as the "Iron Triangle" located just east of Citi Field in Willets Point, Queens. The demolition of the former auto repair shops began in late July to make room for a mixed-use redevelopment project.
